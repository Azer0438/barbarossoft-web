Privacy Policy for Fastlisting AI

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Welcome to Fastlisting AI, operated by BarbarosSoft ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application and connect your Pinterest account.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Pinterest account to Fastlisting AI, we use the official Pinterest API via a secure OAuth connection. We may collect and access the following information:

  • Profile Information: Your username and basic profile details.
  • Pinterest Content: Your boards and pins to facilitate the automated scheduling and publishing process.
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure OAuth tokens provided by Pinterest to keep your account connected. We never see, collect, or store your Pinterest password.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information retrieved via the Pinterest API exclusively to provide the core functionalities of Fastlisting AI, which include:

  • Automating your product listings.
  • Scheduling and publishing pins to your selected boards.
  • Generating AI-powered descriptions and content for your pins.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data or Pinterest data with any third parties. Your data is used strictly for the operation of the Fastlisting AI service.

4. Data Security and Storage

We take the security of your data seriously. Your OAuth tokens and API data are encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Revoking Access and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can disconnect Fastlisting AI from your Pinterest account at any time by visiting your Pinterest account settings (Security and Logins -> Connected Apps) and revoking access.

If you wish to completely delete your account and all associated data from our servers, please contact us directly.
